Water and sewer costs add up — especially with older fixtures that use more gallons per flush than modern models.

## Toilets

Pre-1990s toilets may use **3–5 gallons per flush**. New WaterSense models use **1.28 GPF or less** with comparable performance. Replacing one old toilet can save thousands of gallons per year.

## Showerheads and Faucets

Aerators and low-flow showerheads reduce volume without weak pressure. Look for the WaterSense label.

## Fix Silent Leaks

A running toilet can waste **hundreds of gallons daily**. Dye tablets in the tank can reveal flapper leaks.
