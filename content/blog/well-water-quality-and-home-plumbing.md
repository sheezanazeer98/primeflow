Many homes in rural Northeastern Pennsylvania rely on private wells. Water quality varies by aquifer — and it changes your plumbing maintenance needs.

## Hard Water and Mineral Buildup

High calcium and magnesium leave scale on fixtures, water heaters, and inside pipes. **Water softeners** and periodic descaling extend appliance life.

## Sediment and Pressure Tanks

Sand or silt can wear pump impellers and clog aerators. A **sediment filter** and proper pressure tank maintenance keep flow steady.

## Testing

Test well water annually for bacteria; test for nitrates and other contaminants per local health guidance. Filtration and UV disinfection address many issues.
