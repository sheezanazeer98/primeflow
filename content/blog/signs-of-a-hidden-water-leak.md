Not all leaks show up as a puddle on the floor. Hidden leaks can run for weeks, damaging structure and promoting mold before you see obvious water.

## Watch Your Water Bill

A sudden increase without changed usage often means a leak on the supply side. Check your water meter: if the dial moves when all fixtures are off, water is escaping somewhere.

## Musty Smells or Warped Flooring

Persistent mildew odor, cupping hardwood, or damp carpet near walls may indicate a leak inside the wall or under the slab.

## Sound of Running Water

If you hear water when nothing is turned on, investigate — especially at night when the house is quiet.

## Hot Spots on the Floor

A hot line leak under a slab can warm the floor above. Warm or cold spots deserve professional leak detection.
