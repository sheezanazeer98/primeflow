Natural gas and propane are efficient — but **gas line work is not DIY**. Improper connections cause leaks, fires, and carbon monoxide risk.

## Licensed Professionals

Licensed plumbers handle gas piping to appliances, pressure testing, and shutoff valves per code. **Permits and inspections** protect your family and insurance coverage.

## New Appliances

When you add a gas range, dryer, fireplace, or outdoor kitchen, the line must be sized for **BTU load** and distance from the meter or tank.

## If You Smell Gas

Leave the building immediately, do not flip switches, and call your gas company and 911 from outside. Once the area is safe, call us for repair.
