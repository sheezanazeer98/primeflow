Bathrooms are high-moisture zones — small leaks behind walls or under floors cause mold and rot long before you notice stains on the ceiling below.

## Toilets

Check for **rocking** — loose bolts can break the wax seal and leak sewage. Listen for **running water** after a flush; a failing flapper wastes gallons daily.

## Showers and Tubs

Inspect caulk and grout. Replace cracked caulk to keep water out of wall cavities. Clean drain stoppers regularly to prevent slow drains.

## Under-Sink Cabinets

Once a month, look for moisture, warped particle board, or musty odors. Even a slow drip can damage cabinets.

## Shutoff Valves

Exercise angle stops under sinks and toilets annually — turn off and on so they do not freeze in place when you need them in an emergency.
